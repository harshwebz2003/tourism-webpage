What to Do

Experiences That Last a Lifetime

From jungle safaris to ancient temple trails, Sri Lanka's extraordinary breadth of experiences is unmatched in South Asia.

🐘

Wildlife Safari

Encounter leopards, elephants, and blue whales in their natural habitat across world-class national parks.

🏄

Water Sports

Surf world-class breaks at Arugam Bay, dive coral gardens in Trincomalee, or kite-surf in Kalpitiya.

🍵

Tea Plantation Tour

Walk emerald hillside estates, meet tea pickers, and sip freshly brewed Ceylon tea as clouds drift past.

🛕

Temple Trails

Explore 2,500-year-old dagobas, cave temples ablaze with frescoes, and ancient capitals of forgotten kingdoms.

🍛

Culinary Journey

Devour kottu roti, hoppers, and fiery curries bursting with Sri Lankan spices and coastal seafood.

🧘

Ayurveda Wellness

Restore balance with authentic Ayurvedic treatments and yoga retreats in serene jungle sanctuaries.

🚂

Scenic Train Rides

Board the world-famous Kandy–Ella train winding through cloud forests and emerald valleys.

🪸

Coral Diving

Discover pristine coral reefs, shipwrecks, and kaleidoscopic marine life in crystal-clear waters.
