What to Do

Experiences That Last a Lifetime

From deep jungle tracks to mountain tea estate tours, Sri Lanka delivers an unparalleled range of travel memories.

Wildlife Safari

Observe herds of wild elephants gathering at reservoirs and search for leopards creeping through the dry woodlands.

Coastal Surfing

Catch the famous pipeline breaks at Arugam Bay or cruise calm waves on the southern beach retreats.

Tea Estate Trails

Walk the rolling green valleys of Nuwara Eliya, watch local tea leaves plucked by hand, and enjoy fresh Ceylon brews.

Sacred Heritage

Walk with quiet respect past ancient brick dagobas and cave temples adorned with Buddhist frescoes.

Local Cuisine

Savor fresh hot hoppers, coconut pol sambol, and coastal fish curry prepared with hand-ground spices.

Ayurvedic Spas

Indulge in authentic traditional herbal oil massages and custom wellness treatments in jungle sanctuaries.

Highland Rail

Peer out the windows of the historic colonial train winding through cloud-forested peaks between Kandy and Ella.

Coral Diving

Swim above colorful shallow reefs and historic ocean shipwrecks in the warm tropical seas.

Best Time to Visit

Two Monsoons, Infinite Sun

Sri Lanka has two distinct weather zones. When one coast is rainy, the other is bright and perfect for surfing or diving.

Dec - Apr

Peak South/West

  • Blue sky on Southern beaches
  • Whales migrating off Mirissa
  • Excellent cultural climate
  • Little rainfall, bright days
Best: Galle, Mirissa, Colombo
May - Sep

East Coast Season

  • Calm waters in Trincomalee
  • Surf season at Arugam Bay
  • Misty hill valleys lush green
  • Great off-peak price drops
Best: Trincomalee, Arugam Bay
Oct - Nov

Shoulder Months

  • Gentle inter-monsoon periods
  • Great wildlife gathering sights
  • Fewer travelers on trails
  • Lush rainforest hiking guides
Best: Yala, Kandy, Nuwara Eliya

Year-round holiday paradise - Sri Lanka's dual tropical currents mean there is always a sunny beach waiting for you.
