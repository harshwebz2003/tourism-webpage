What to Do

Experiences That Last a Lifetime

From jungle safaris to ancient temple trails, Sri Lanka's extraordinary breadth of experiences is unmatched in South Asia.

🐘

Wildlife Safari

Encounter wild leopards, elephant herds, and blue whales in their natural habitats across national reserves.

🏄

Water Sports

Surf world-class breaks at Arugam Bay, dive coral gardens in Trincomalee, or kite-surf in Kalpitiya.

🍵

Tea Estate Tours

Walk emerald hillside plantations, meet tea pickers, and sip freshly brewed Ceylon tea as clouds drift past.

🛕

Temple Trails

Explore 2,500-year-old dagobas, cave temples ablaze with frescoes, and ancient forest monasteries.

🍛

Culinary Journeys

Devour kottu roti, hoppers, and coconut sambols bursting with aromatic local spices and coastal seafood.

🧘

Ayurveda & Yoga

Restore natural balance with authentic Ayurvedic treatments and yoga retreats in serene jungle spas.

🚂

Scenic Train Journeys

Board the famous blue train winding through cloud forests and emerald valleys of the hill country.

🪸

Coral Diving

Discover pristine coral reefs, ancient marine shipwrecks, and kaleidoscopic schools of fish.
